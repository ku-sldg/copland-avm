The traces associated with a state.

Inductive traceS: St → list Ev → Prop :=
| tstop: ∀ p e,
    traceS (stop p e) []
| tconf: ∀ t tr p e,
    trace t p tr →
    traceS (conf t p e) tr
| trem: ∀ st tr j p,
    traceS st tr →
    traceS (rem j p st)
           (tr ++
               [(rpy (pred j) p (pl st))])
| tls: ∀ st tr1 t tr2,
    traceS st tr1 →
    trace t (pl st) tr2 →
    traceS (ls st t) (tr1 ++ tr2)
.

Fixpoint esizeS s:=
  match s with
  | stop _ _ ⇒ 0
  | conf t _ _ ⇒ esize t
  | rem _ _ st ⇒ 1 + esizeS st
  | ls st t ⇒ esizeS st + esize t
  
  end.

A trace of the LTS is a trace of the big-step semantics.

Lemma lstar_trace:
  ∀ t p e tr,
    well_formed_r t →
    lstar (conf t p e) tr (stop p (aeval t p e)) →
    trace t p tr.

The key theorem.

Theorem ordered:
  ∀ t p e tr ev0 ev1,
    well_formed_r t →
    lstar (conf t p e) tr (stop p (aeval t p e)) →
    prec (ev_sys t p) ev0 ev1 →
    earlier tr ev0 ev1.
